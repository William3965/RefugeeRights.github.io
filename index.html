Refugees have been going on for a very long time. No matter in what era, there is always a conflict. The war and violence cause many people to flee their country and the process has been around for a very long time. One of the first record of refugee was from 1687 (Huegunots) many flee their home to escape persecutions and since then it has been growing in numbers. Until the world wars where we saw the most number of refugees ever, this resolves in the United nations felt the need to create international guidelines relating to protection and acceptance of refugees. Then an amendment was made in 1967 to increased the scope of the document to include refugees fleeing any life-threatening situation worldwide, rather than relating exclusively to pre-1951 conflicts in Europe. With all the guidelines and help from many refugees still faces a lot of problems.
